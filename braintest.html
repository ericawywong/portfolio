BrainTest

A clinically proven application for screening early stages of Alzheimer's

Alzheimer’s is a chronic ongoing condition. It is an untreatable condition that will negatively affect memory, thinking, and behavior. Based on research, it mostly affects those who are aged 65 and over. Yet, there are also signs that show Alzheimer’s symptoms are showing at an earlier age. BrainTest is able to help and support doctors to identify any Alzheimer’s symptoms at their onset.

Gain peace of mind

Alzheimer's is a progressive disease. At the beginning, there might not be obvious symptoms or symptoms might be very minimal. For those who suspect your close ones might be diagnosed with Alzheimer's, you can have access to the test and gain peace of mind.

Interactive Guide

The Interactive Guide is an educational information piece to help users and their family members to learn more about Alzheimer’s and the ways to help prevent it. To make it more interesting, we decided to make it more colorful and interesting by creating illustrations and animations.
