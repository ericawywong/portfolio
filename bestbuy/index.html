Description

As a member of North America's largest and fastest-growing retail company, I played a key role in producing weekly digital newsletters. I collaborated with stakeholders, revamped the layout, and streamlined the design to create impactful promotions and enhance the overall design process.

Role

  • UI Design
  • Concepting
  • Production Design
  • Coordination
  • Documentation
  • Localization
  • A/B Testing

Why redesign?

As the digital landscape evolved, adapting to mobile design became imperative.

The existing newsletter, designed for larger screens, posed challenges with its crowded layout, extensive copy, and small images, hindering user engagement on smaller screens.

Ideas

After doing some research:

  • Implemented a streamlined design to enhance quick user comprehension.
  • Ensured consistent branding with the existing e-commerce site for a unified user experience.
  • Optimized content by removing redundant text, focusing on essential keywords for clear and impactful communication.
  • Introduced dynamic motion graphics to boost audience engagement.
  • Enhanced product visibility by enlarging images and interactive areas, improving mobile usability.

A/B Testing

Skateholders agreed to do A/B testing to see if my idea and theory was correct. We contacted the 3rd party agent and asked them to make 2 versions of the email, evenly disturbuted to 2 even groups of the email subscribers within Canada.
