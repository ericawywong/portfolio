Titanic Exhibition — SkyTrain Promotional Wrap

I designed a Titanic-themed full-train wrapper for SkyTrain, Vancouver’s urban public transit system. The design aimed to promote the Titanic exhibition and educate commuters about the event through striking visuals and curated information panels.

By transforming an everyday commute into an immersive experience, the wrapper captured passengers’ attention, building excitement and awareness for the exhibition across the city.
