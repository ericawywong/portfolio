About Me

Hi, I’m Erica Wong — a product designer with a love for solving messy problems and turning them into clear, intuitive experiences.

I earned my BA in Interactive Arts & Technology from Simon Fraser University and began my career at a design agency in Hong Kong. The experience sharpened my skills and exposed me to diverse projects, but I eventually returned home to Vancouver — I missed my family, the city, and yes, even the rain 🌧️.

Since then, I’ve worked across industries — from fast-paced retail and e-commerce to tech startups and established companies — designing everything from responsive websites to mobile apps to playful in-game collectibles.

Outside of work, I’m a proud cat parent to Gatsby & Vessi, enjoy drawing, a good movie / show (favorite: Nightmare Before Christmas 🎃, Dexter 🩸), and always tea over coffee.

A Raincouver resident originally from Hong Kong, I speak both Cantonese and Mandarin and bring that cross-cultural perspective into my work.

Thanks for stopping by 🙂.
