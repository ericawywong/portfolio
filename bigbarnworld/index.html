Responsive Design for Small Screens

Big Barn World is available on both desktop and mobile platforms. Ensuring the artwork is visible and effective on small screens was crucial.

The smallest size, 75x75 pixels, required clear silhouettes of characters to allow players to easily identify different animals at a glance.

Designing Adults & Babies

One of the enjoyable aspects of this project was designing both adult and baby versions of each animal. The concept is that players can grow their animals or get help from friends, allowing the animals on their farm to grow and develop over time.
